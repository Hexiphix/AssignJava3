How To Begin? Step 1 - Discover What People Need!

Image from Hover

Not everything is in need of a tutorial, and many things already have lots of tutorials, so the first thing any good tutorial must do, is to discover and cover things that seem like they need it. If you are making a tutorial for something not released yet, or something you had difficulty getting all the right information on, then you may already have the right topic, but otherwise, research can help.
